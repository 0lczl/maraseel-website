Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?
Shipping times vary based on the service selected and destination. Express services typically deliver within 1-3 business days, while standard services take 5-7 business days. International shipments may take 7-14 business days depending on customs clearance.
What items are prohibited for shipping?
Prohibited items include hazardous materials, flammable substances, perishable foods without proper packaging, illegal items, and items restricted by destination country laws. Please check our terms and conditions for a complete list.
How can I track my shipment?
You can track your shipment using the tracking number provided in your confirmation email. Simply enter the tracking number on our tracking page for real-time updates on your shipment's status and location.
Do you offer insurance for shipments?
Yes, all shipments include basic insurance coverage. Additional insurance can be purchased for high-value items. Contact our customer service team for more information about insurance options.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and corporate accounts. Payment is typically required at the time of booking, though account customers may have credit terms available.
